# Official Zoom Studios

Welcome to the official portal and documentation of **Zoom Studios**. We are an independent developer collective building high-performance software, custom game engines, artificial intelligence systems, and next-generation operating systems from the ground up.

* **Core Mission:** Engineering high-performance software, native tools, and advanced operating systems.
* **Official Organization:** [Zoom Studios Real PC on GitHub](https://github.com/Zoom-Studios-Real-Pc)

### Ecosystem Overview
Our development ecosystem focuses on multiple pillars:
* **Applications & Tools:** Custom desktop utilities, localization managers, and productivity software.
* **Game Engines & Development:** Custom-built rendering pipelines and engines for high-performance gaming.
* **AI & Operating Systems:** Experimental and production-ready system software and AI-driven environments.
